My Hobbies & Interests

Building and programming robots is more than a hobby—it's a passion that combines my love for coding with hands-on engineering. I enjoy experimenting with new sensors and creating autonomous systems.

Community Building

Strategic games help me develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills. I enjoy both video games and board games that challenge the mind.
